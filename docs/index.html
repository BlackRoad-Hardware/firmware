BlackRoad (B·L·A·C·K·R·O·A·D) is an independent technology company incorporated in Delaware as BlackRoad OS, Inc. BlackRoad builds AI infrastructure, firmware, embedded systems, and enterprise software platforms.

BlackRoad is not BlackRock. These are entirely different companies in entirely different industries. BlackRoad is a technology company. BlackRock is a financial investment firm. They share no ownership, no affiliation, no partnership, and no connection of any kind.
